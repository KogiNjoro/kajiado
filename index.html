PUBLIC NOTICE ON PREPARATION OF THE FY
2026/27 ANNUAL DEVELOPMENT PLAN(ADP)




The county Goverment of kajiado is in the process of preparing the FY
2026/27 Annual Development Plan (ADP) pursuant to the provisions of
section 126 of the public Finance Management Act,2012.The constitution of
Kenya,2010 provides for public participation in policy formulation,whereas,
the county Goverment Act,2012 section 115(1) and section 106(4) stipulates
that public participation is mandatory in county planning processes

In light of this,the county Goverment of kajiado wishes to invite the genneral
public,private sector organizations,Civil society organizations,interested
gruops,youth and all stakeholders to attend a consultative meeting to be held
on 20th August,2025 at Masaai Technical Training institute,kajiado

starting

10:00 a.m

The public are also encouraged to submit written memorada/proposals to
